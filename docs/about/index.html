About This Blog

Franz AI Blog covers practical AI tools, workflows, and strategies that help businesses work smarter. No hype, no fluff -- just actionable insights on how AI is changing the way we work.

About Franz Enzenhofer

Franz Enzenhofer is a digital strategist, author, and AI tools expert based in Vienna, Austria. He is the founder of Full Stack Optimization, where he helps companies understand and adopt AI-powered workflows.

With over 15 years of experience in digital strategy and technology, Franz focuses on the intersection of AI and business -- finding the tools and approaches that deliver real results, not just impressive demos.

He speaks regularly at conferences about AI adoption, runs hands-on workshops for business teams, and writes about the tools that are actually worth using.

  • How companies are integrating AI into their workflows
  • Practical comparisons of AI services and platforms
  • Strategies for adopting AI without the hype
